The coefficient of variation versus mean plots (HIV+ and HIV- patients)

Kushal K Dey

8/1/2017

In this script, we plot the coefficient of variation against the mean expression patterns of the genes for each study case (HIV+ and HIV-). We know that the points that act as outliers in this plot do not fit the Poisson model well. We here try to observe if there is a pattern among the genes that show large deviations from this model. Are these genes related? Are they part of the same functional group or pathway?
